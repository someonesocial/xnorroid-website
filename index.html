Lüftungsapp (Ventilation App)

A simple app to maintain and record regular ventilation times.

Well-ventilated rooms with plenty of fresh air are important, whether for learning in schools, productive work in companies, or for your well-being at home!

This app reminds you to ventilate your rooms at self-defined intervals. It permanently displays a timer in your Android device's notifications showing the remaining time until the next ventilation process. Afterward, you can confirm the process, and the next time period timer starts.

To ensure that ventilation has been carried out regularly, the exact time and date of each ventilation phase is stored on your device. You can access this data in the app and also export it as a CSV file to transfer it to a spreadsheet program.

You can find the exported data under: Internal shared storage -> Download -> Lueftungsapp_Daten.csv

Download Lüftungsapp on Google Play
